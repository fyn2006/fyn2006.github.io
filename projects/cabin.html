Cabin Project

This project involved designing and building a cabin through a step-by-step construction process, from planning and site preparation to framing, finishing, and final detailing.

The project focused on practical construction, material planning, structural assembly, and hands-on problem solving.

The cabin project consisted of six main steps:

  1. Planning the design and preparing materials
  2. Preparing the site and foundation
  3. Building the floor and base structure
  4. Framing the walls and roof
  5. Installing exterior panels and weather protection
  6. Completing finishing details and final assembly
